Lesson 8




MOVIE BUFF (CLICK TO OPEN)

Exercise 4 text

Paul is a big movie buff. He heard about a good horror movie, so he invited Carol to go with him and his brother to see it at the theater. Carol is not really keen on theaters, so she asked him to come instead to her place to watch the movie with some other friends. Paul accepted, but asked Carol not to watch the trailer because he wanted her to be surprised. Carol promised that she wouldn’t.


Past Continuous/Progressive Tense


Pozitivna forma:


Subjekat + pomoćni glagol WAS/WERE + glagol + ING


The sun was shining every day that summer.

As I spoke, the children were laughing at my cleverness.


Negativna forma:


Subjekat + WAS/WERE + NOT + glagol + ING

John was not going to London.


Upitna forma:


WAS/WERE + Subjekat + glagol

Was I sitting in that restaurant last night?

Were they coming towards the car?
