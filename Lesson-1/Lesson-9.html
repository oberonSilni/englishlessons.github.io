SIGHTSEEING (CLICK TO OPEN)

Exercise 4 text

Carol spent her weekend on holiday in Madrid. She went to see her old friend and found some time to go sightseeing. She saw some amazing sights. Luckily, she remembered to bring her camera with her so she took a lot of photos to show them to her colleagues. She took some time to buy souvenirs for her friends and decided to send a postcard to Paul, who is a postcard collector.
