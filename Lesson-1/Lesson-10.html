Lesson 10




AT THE BAR (CLICK TO OPEN)

Exercise 4 text

After work one day, Carol and Paul were at the bar discussing their new boss. Carol thought that the new boss was not as good as their previous one. He seemed confused and less organized. Paul agreed, but added that the new boss might just need some time to learn the ropes. Finally, they agreed to have a few more drinks because in the future, they might not have time.

Future Simple Tense

Future Simple Tense (Prosto buduće vreme) se gradi od glagola “will” i osnovnog oblika glagola (infinitiv).

Pozitivna forma:
I will (I'll) speak.

Upitna forma:
Will I speak?

Negativna forma:
I will not (won't) speak.
