Simple present tense


Pozitivna forma:

Subjekat + glagol (s, es, ies u sluƒçaju da je subjekat he/she/it)

I go there every day.

He goes there every day.


Negativna forma:

Subjekat + do/does (za he/she/it)+ NOT + glagol

I do not (don't) go there.

He does not (doesn't) go there.


Upitna forma:

Do/does + subjekat + glagol

Do I go there?

Does he go there?



Present continuous tense


Pozitivna forma:

Subjekat + am/is/are + glagol+ING

I am going there right now.


Negativna forma:

Subjekat + am/is/are + NOT + glagol+ING

I am not going there.


Upitna forma:

Am/is/are + subjekat + glagol+ING

Am I going there?
