Past simple



Past action - I ate at lunch time.
Past habit, repeated action - I played the violin when I was young.

Statement: I had a car.
Question: Did I have a car?
Negative: I didn't have a car.

Present simple



Habit, repeated action - I eat bacon every day.
Facts - The sun rises
Schedule future event - My plane lands at 11:20

Statement: The sun rises every day.
Question: Does the sun rise every day?
Negative: It doesnt rise every day.

Future simple



Future action - I will eat at lunchtime.

Statement: The sun will rise tomorrow.
Question: Will the sun rise tomorrow?
Negative: The sun will not rise tomorrow.
