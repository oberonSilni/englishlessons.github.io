BUYING GROCERIES (CLICK TO OPEN)

Exercise 4 text

Carol is planning her birthday party, so she went to the newly opened supermarket to buy some beverages and groceries for a dinner she plans to make for her friends. The store clerk helped Carol shop for vegetables, spices, and drinks. Carol's bill came to $47; she gave the cashier a $50 bill, and got back $3 change.
