PHOTO EDITING IS FUN (CLICK TO OPEN)

Exercise 4 text

Carol and Paul finally had a day-off, so they sat down and looked at the photos Carol had taken on her business trip. Paul opened his photo editing software and started retouching, cropping, and resizing the photos. He edited the photo Carol took with the Chrysler Building to make it look like Carol is a giant holding the building in her hand. After the editing process, Carol's photos looked even better.

First Conditional

Used to talk about things which might happen in the future.
Of course, we can't know what will happen in the future, but this describes possible things, which could easily come true.
