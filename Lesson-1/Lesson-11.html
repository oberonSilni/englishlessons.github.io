IT'S SUMMERTIME (CLICK TO OPEN)

Exercise 4 text

Carol and Paul are dreaming about nice weather, as it has been cold for several days. They are talking about what they like doing during the summertime. Paul usually spends time in nature whenever he has free time, and enjoys the greenery. He also likes hiking in the mountains. Carol spends most of her leisure time with her friends having picnics and going to the pool to cool off. They both agree that the summer is their favorite season.
