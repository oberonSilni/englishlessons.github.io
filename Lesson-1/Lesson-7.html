COMPUTER SAVVY (CLICK TO OPEN)

Exercise 4 text

Carol was having some issues with her computer, so she asked her computer savvy friend, Paul, to come to her rescue. She explained that while she was surfing the internet, her computer shut down. When Paul arrived, he realized that nothing too serious had happened and only needed to make some updates to the programs on Carol's computer. As soon as he finished updating the programs, Carol's PC was up and running again. Paul then downloaded some anti-virus software on to Carol's computer.
