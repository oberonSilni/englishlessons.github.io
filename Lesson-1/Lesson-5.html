WALKING ON A CHILLY DAY (CLICK TO OPEN)



Exercise 4 Listening comprehension text:

Carol was getting ready for work and wasn’t sure what to wear because she didn’t know what the weather was like. She called Paul to ask him, and he told her that it was very warm at that moment, but that the weather forecast said it might rain during the night. Carol decided to wear something light and bring a coat, just in case.




Past Simple Tense



Pozitivna forma:


Subjekat + glagol + ED (or irregular verb)

I tried that yesterday.

He went there last month.


Negativna forma:


Subjekat + did (prošlo vreme pomoćnog DO) + NOT + glagol (čist glagol, bez ED ili nepravilne forme)

I did not (didn't) go there.


Upitna forma:


Did + subjekat + glagol(čist)

Did he go there?


Past Continuous/Progressive Tense


Pozitivna forma:


Subjekat + pomoćni glagol WAS/WERE + glagol + ING


The sun was shining every day that summer.

As I spoke, the children were laughing at my cleverness.


Negativna forma:


Subjekat + WAS/WERE + NOT + glagol + ING

John was not going to London.


Upitna forma:


WAS/WERE + Subjekat + glagol

Was I there yesterday?

Were you there yesterday?
